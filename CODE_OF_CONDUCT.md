# CODE OF CONDUCT


## **0. About itself**

We are willing to discuss amendments from people in good faith and intentions.

We are not open to discussing the validity of each item, nor obligated to discuss it.

It is intently worded without shield of boringness.



## **1. This app is made by disabled, neurodivergent, trans, plural people**

Everyone with good faith and no bad intent is welcome. We want to serve communities, and we love new faces.

Inclusivity is a social contract. Be whatever but be nice or be kicked.



## **2. No AI contribution**

AI is a direct contradiction with the first item. If you want to fund monsters openly stating their bad intentions, tools made to disempower and replace, and abhorrent electricity and water usage, to provide half-cooked diarrhea then you broke the contract.

It is not in good faith, it is not morally, socially or environmentally neutral.

If we even get a whiff of AI by checking your other code, we'll consider it an attempt of hiding it from us in your PR.

  a) Is included: If you openly use AI generated pictures, like those piss-tinted pantomimes of Ghibli, then you are out, i dont want anything to do with you.

  b) Is NOT included: Assistive, focused AI such as DeepL for translations for the sake of communicating, or any similar assistive technology (such as for using a computer for legally blind people), are OK.

We want to foster community. That comes from people. Machines do not provide community.
